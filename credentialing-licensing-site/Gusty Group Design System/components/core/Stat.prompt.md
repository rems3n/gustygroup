**Stat** — big EB Garamond metric over a muted sans caption. Group 2–3 in a row divided by a hairline rule.

```jsx
<Stat value="$25M+" label="Raised, seed through Series A" />
<Stat value="0 → 40" label="Employees built and managed" tone="on-dark" />
```

Use `tone="on-dark"` on olive backgrounds.
