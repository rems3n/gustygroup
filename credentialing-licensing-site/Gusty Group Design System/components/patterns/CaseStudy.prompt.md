**CaseStudy** — two-column track-record entry (meta column + body/stats), tuned for the olive "Selected work" surface. Composes Tag and Stat.

```jsx
<CaseStudy
  tag="Flagship · 2018 — 2026"
  title="Mend Labs"
  sub="Health tech startup. Founder served as COO, founding through Series A."
  stats={[{value:'$25M+',label:'Raised, seed through Series A'},{value:'0 → 40',label:'Employees built'}]}>
  <p>Joined Mend at the founding stage…</p>
</CaseStudy>
```

`tone="on-dark"` (default) for olive sections; `tone="default"` on paper.
