What we manage.

The complete credentialing lifecycle, prioritized by the revenue each provider unlocks.

01Roster & payer matrix

We build a full provider-by-payer matrix and prioritize it by revenue-at-risk, so the highest-value credentialing happens first.

02CAQH build, attest & maintenance

CAQH profiles created, attested, and re-attested on schedule, with documents kept current so payers never stall on stale data.

03Primary-source verification

License, DEA, board certification, education, and NPI verified at the source and packaged the way payers expect.

30-day monitoring, automated recredentialing, and a live client portal showing the status of every provider and payer.
