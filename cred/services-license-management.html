Solutions · License management

Keep every provider licensed, in every state they practice.

Gusty Group manages the full licensing lifecycle — new-state applications, renewals, DEA/CDS registrations, and compact eligibility — so a new hire is ready to practice and no existing provider ever lapses.

What we manage.

End-to-end ownership of every license, in every jurisdiction your providers touch.

01New-state license applications

We prepare, submit, and shepherd state medical, nursing, and allied-health board applications — including all supporting documentation and board correspondence.

02Renewal tracking & timely filing

Every active license is tracked to its renewal date and filed well ahead of the deadline, so a provider is never caught practicing on an expired license.

03DEA / CDS registration & renewal

Federal DEA and state controlled-substance registrations obtained and renewed, with address and location updates handled as your practice grows.

04Compact license eligibility & maintenance

We assess IMLC, NLC, and other compact eligibility, secure privileges, and maintain them as your providers add states.

A dedicated team, one source of truth.

Your providers, licenses, and payer applications tracked in one place — with the next action and its owner always clear, so nothing slips between hand-offs.
